Late-night cravings, sonic choices

Late at night, when most of the campus is asleep, hungry NYUAD students find themselves craving something to eat after a long study session. They decide to visit the convenience store in search of the perfect midnight snack. The story invites the audience to participate by helping the students choose what to eat based on different sound cues. Through this project, our goal is to explore how sound can guide perception and decision-making, while creating an engaging and relatable interactive experience for students.
